# Tech-Job-Market-

## Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of the data analytics job market from 2017 to 2021. By analyzing over 9,000 job postings across 8 unique data-related roles, the project identifies key industry trends, salary expectations based on experience, and the most highly demanded technical skills. This tool is designed to help recruitment agencies and data professionals make data-driven career and hiring decisions.

## Key Market KPIs & Metrics
* **Total Job Postings Analyzed:** 9,000
* **Average Salary:** $8.75K 
* **Average Years of Experience Required:** 4.5 Years
* **Unique Data Job Titles Tracked:** 8 (e.g., Data Engineer, Data Scientist, Business Analyst)

## Actionable Market Insights
* **Technical Skill Dominance:** SQL (19.27%) and Python (18.07%) are the absolute foundational skills required in the data job market, followed closely by Cloud and AWS infrastructure knowledge.
* **Seniority Demand:** The market shows a strong preference for "Mid-Senior level" professionals, representing the largest segment (55.74%) of total job postings.
* **Industry Concentration:** The "Information Technology" and "Internet" sectors are the primary drivers of data-related employment, indicating where recruitment efforts should be focused.
